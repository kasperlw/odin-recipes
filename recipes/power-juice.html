Power Juice

Description

Simple yet tasty and healthy true juice made with only green ingredients to boost the green power!

Ingredients

  • 50g Baby Spinach
  • 4 green apples, halved
  • Cucumber
  • One lime
  • One (1 inch) piece of fresh ginger

STEPS

  1. cut all ingredients in hefty chunks
  2. Process everything through a juicer
  3. Serve immediately, or store in a glass jar in the fridge; shake before drinking
  4. Feel The Power Growing From The Inside
